My Story

I'd like to consider myself as a mirror, reflecting life back at itself with what I create. My personal interpretation of life inspires my work.

I'm passionate about learning and constantly improving oneself. I'm inquisitive and love to find new ways to challenge myself and expand my knowledge, not to lorde it, I enjoy it out of the simple excitement of discovery. I grew up in a very spiritual household, my parents came from very modest beginnings and weren't formally educated, but they still strove to make the best of their situation. This dedication in combination with faith and determination has been imparted on me as well. Because of these elements i'm tenacious and don't faulter under pressure. I've spent most of my working career trying to up-skill myself as much as possible, especially in my free time. I enjoy learning and find it to be a good confidence booster when I finally grasp a concept. As busy as I am there's also a polar oppsoite to my personality in that I also enjoy doing nothing and being stationary. This gives me time to reflect on where I am and what i've accomplished, I firmly believe that resting is just as important as doing.
